Lasagne

Description

A staple dish favoured by many households for its rich flavour and vesatility.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Minced meat
  • Cheese of choice
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh cream
  • Basil, chopped

Steps

  1. Pre-heat your oven to 150C
  2. Cook the mince
  3. Add your tomatoes/ tomatoe sauce
  4. Add the pasta sheets to oven safe bowl
  5. Add the mince meat in layers
  6. Add your cream sauce
  7. Add your cheese on top
  8. Bake for 30 minutes on medium heat
