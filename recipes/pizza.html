Cheesy Pizza

Description

This is a pizza recipe for a very cheesy pizza. To add some variety, you can add your own ingredients.

Please see the ingredients list below for more detail:

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Oil
  • Tomato Puree
  • Water
  • cheddar
  • Mozzarella
  • fresh tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make your dough using the flour, oil, and eggs.
  2. Let the dough rest for 1 hour
  3. Flatten the dough to form your base
  4. Spread the tomato puree on top of the pizza
  5. Add your cheddar, then your mozzarella (grated)
  6. Add your choice ingredients
  7. Salt to taste
  8. Bake in pizza oven for 10 minutes, or until the base is crispy brown
